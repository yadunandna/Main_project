## Sample Test Cases

### Daily Check-In

#### Test Case 1 – Academic Stress

```text
I studied for 10 hours today but my mock test score was lower than expected. I keep comparing myself to my friends and I am worried about my JEE rank.
```

#### Test Case 2 – Burnout Risk

```text
I have been studying 12 hours every day for the last week. I sleep only 5 hours and feel exhausted. I cannot concentrate anymore.
```

#### Test Case 3 – Positive Day

```text
Today was productive. I completed my study targets and felt confident during practice tests. I also took breaks and exercised.
```

#### Test Case 4 – Exam Anxiety

```text
My exam is next month and I feel nervous all the time. Even when I study, I worry that I will forget everything during the exam.
```

#### Test Case 5 – Time Management Issues

```text
I wasted a lot of time scrolling through social media today. Now I feel guilty because I could not complete my study plan.
```

#### Test Case 6 – Sleep Deprivation

```text
I stayed awake until 3 AM studying chemistry. I am feeling tired, irritable, and unable to focus today.
```

#### Test Case 7 – Family Pressure

```text
My parents keep asking about my marks and college admissions. I feel pressured and stressed whenever these conversations happen.
```

#### Test Case 8 – Peer Comparison

```text
My friends are getting much better scores in mock tests than me. I feel like I am falling behind even though I study every day.
```

#### Test Case 9 – Lack of Motivation

```text
I don't feel motivated to study anymore. Every day feels repetitive and I struggle to start my study sessions.
```

#### Test Case 10 – Severe Emotional Distress

```text
I feel hopeless. Nothing seems to be improving and I don't know how to handle this pressure anymore.
```
